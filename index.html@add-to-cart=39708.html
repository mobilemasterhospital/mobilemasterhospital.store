located in Shenyang City, China. We’ re approved by the Medical Administration Bureau and registered in Industrial and Commercial Administration Bureau. Services

Our products are widely used in the medical industry.

The series products are extensively used in hospital medical treatment, health colleges and universities, laboratories, and health inspections.
